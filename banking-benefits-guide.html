How to Avoid Them

Switch to Online

Most online banks have $0 overdraft and $0 maintenance fees.

Direct Deposits

Set up at least $500 monthly to waive traditional bank fees.

Eco-Friendly

Opt for e-statements to instantly remove paper statement fees.

Banking Apps & Technology

Mobile Check Deposits

Deposit checks from anywhere just by snapping a photo in your app.

Real-Time Alerts

Instantly get notified of any transaction or low balance alert.

Digital Wallets

Link your cards to Apple Pay or Google Pay for touchless payments.

Enhanced Security

Use biometric login (FaceID) and instant card lock features.

Pro Tips to Maximize Banking Benefits

Automation is Key

Automate your savings transfers the day after payday to ensure your wealth grows before you spend it.

Review & Switch

Don't stay with a bank providing 0.1% interest. Review rates every 6 months and switch to keep the best APY.

Signup Bonuses

Churning bank bonuses can net you $2k+ per year just by splitting your direct deposits between new accounts.

Negotiate Fees

Call your bank if you get hit with a fee. Often, if you're a loyal customer, they will waive it as a one-time courtesy.
