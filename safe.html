Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the USA Academy Program.

Is this a government program?
No. The USA Academy is an independent educational initiative. We are not affiliated with, authorized, or endorsed by any government agency. We provide educational tools to help you navigate public systems that already exist.
Do you take a commission from my funds?
Absolutely not. Unlike "recovery agents" who may charge 30-50% of your restitution, we only charge a one-time educational fee. Once you learn the system, 100% of any funds you recover belong to you.
How long does the training take?
The core modules can be completed in approximately 2 hours. However, the materials and templates are yours to keep and use at your own pace. Most students complete their first official inquiry within 48 hours of starting.
Is there a guarantee?
Yes. We offer a 60-day satisfaction period managed by Digistore24. If you feel the educational materials do not provide the value promised, you can request a full refund according to their policies.
